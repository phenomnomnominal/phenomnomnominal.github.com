About Us


Welcome to Coastlands Dental Health Centre

We are a family-oriented practice based at Coastlands in Paraparaumu and have been proudly helping the Kapiti Coast to look after their oral health for several decades.

Our professional team of New Zealand trained dentists and dental hygienists are capable of helping with all your dental needs, backed up by our fantastic team of support staff.

We know that planning a visit to the dentist can make you a bit nervous - especially if it's been a while! Rest assured that our experienced team will be able to help with any questions or concerns you have, and help create a treatment plan that is best for you.

It is very normal to feel a bit nervous, so do talk to our friendly reception staff and your dentist if you are worried. We will usually recommend a comprehensive check-up and allow time to talk and plan the most appropraite treatement for you. You may be pleasantly surprised to find out how comfortable most procedures are these days!

Dental Department


Dental Department

Everybody wants to have an amazing smile, and our dentists pride themselves on their ability to help you achieve that!

There are many ways that we can help you achieve that perfect smile. Whitening and tidying up those "not so white" fillings can achieve wonders, but we can also advise you about the many exciting options of bleaching, composite bonding and veneers, porcelain crowns, facings and onlays, all of which may not cost as much as you might expect!

Quality dental care can be quite an investment but our experienced front office staff are happy to discuss payment options with you so you can afford the treatment you need. Your dentist will give you an estimated quote once a full oral health examination has been completed.

Our dentists are able to assist you with a wide range of dental services including:

  • White fillings
  • Tooth whitening
  • Crowns and bridges
  • Veneers and onlays
  • Tooth extractions
  • Root canal treatment
  • Dentures and partial plates
  • Treatment of children
  • ACC treatment

Hygiene Department


Hygiene Department

Dental hygienists treat gum diseases and educate people about how to prevent oral disease through care of their teeth and gums.

Our hygienists are registered professionals who are very experienced at treating gum disease by gently scaling and cleaning your teeth. They are also enthusiastic about educating you on the most effective ways to keep your teeth and gums healthy.

A trip to the hygienist can make a huge long term difference in keeping your teeth and gums healthy. They will advise you on the many aspects of dental hygiene, including the use of toothbrushs (electric or regular), interdental brushes, electronic devices, dental floss, rinses and topical pastes.

Our dentists will often recommend a visit to the hygienist if you have any worries at all about:

  • Bleeding guns
  • Swollen or inflamed gums
  • Bad breath
  • Loose teeth
  • Sensitive teeth
  • Darkening teeth
  • Receding gums
  • Effects of smoking
  • Dry mouth

You can also make an appointment directly with one of our hygienists.
