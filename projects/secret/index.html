About Us


Welcome to Coastlands Dental Health Centre

We are a family-oriented general dentistry practice based at Coastlands in Paraparaumu and have been privileged in helping Kapiti Coast residents with their oral health for over twenty years.

Our professional team of experienced dentists and hygienists, and our visiting orthodontist are trained and skilled in helping with your dental treatment needs, backed up by our fantastic team of support staff.

We understand that planning a visit to the dentist can often cause anxiety and many people feel a bit nervous, especially if it's been a while since their last visit. We know it is often difficult to even take that first step in making an appointment. Please be assured, we are non-judgemental, respectful, caring and gentle in our approach to helping you achieve optimum oral health.

Do phone and talk to our friendly reception staff and let them know if you are at all worried. They will listen carefully in assessing your situation and help answer any questions or concerns you may have. Depending on your treatment needs they will schedule time accordingly and may recommend a comprehensive examination allowing for the dentist to talk through various options in planning the most appropriate treatment for you. You may even be pleasantly surprised to find how comfortable most procedures are today due to advances in technology and materials.

Dental Department


Dental Department

Everybody wants to have an amazing smile, and our dentists pride themselves on their ability to help you achieve that!

There are many ways that we can help you achieve that perfect smile. Whitening and tidying up those "not so white" fillings can achieve wonders, but we can also advise you about the many exciting options of bleaching, composite bonding and veneers, porcelain crowns, facings and onlays, all of which may not cost as much as you might expect!

Quality dental care can be quite an investment but our experienced front office staff are happy to discuss payment options with you so you can afford the treatment you need. Your dentist will give you an estimated quote once a full oral health examination has been completed.

Our dentists are able to assist you with a wide range of dental services including:

  • White fillings
  • Tooth whitening
  • Crowns and bridges
  • Veneers and onlays
  • Tooth extractions
  • Root canal treatment
  • Dentures and partial plates
  • Treatment of children
  • ACC treatment

Hygiene Department


Hygiene Department

Having a healthy mouth is important for overall health and well-being. Dental hygienists assess gum health, treat gum diseases by removing hard plaque and educate people about how to prevent oral disease through maintenance and home care of their teeth and gums.

Our hygienists are registered health professionals who are experienced and highly trained in helping patients achieve improved higher levels of dental health. A visit to the hygienist can make a huge difference in keeping your teeth and gums healthy.

Our hygienist will advise and work with you on the skills, tools and techniques you can use to control and effectively remove dental plaque to improve your oral health.

Our dentists will often recommend a visit to the hygienist if there are any concerns about:

  • Bleeding guns
  • Swollen or inflamed gums
  • Receding gums
  • Sensitive teeth
  • Loose teeth
  • Dry mouth
  • Bad breath
  • Stained teeth
  • Darkening teeth
  • Effects of smoking

You can also make an appointment directly with one of our hygienists.
